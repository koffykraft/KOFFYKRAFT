Self-study

How to Use This Roasting Course

Use each lesson as a learning loop: read, observe, write, taste, and review. Do not move only by page count; move when the idea is usable.

The learning loop

  1. Read the lesson objective and terms before the main section.
  2. Write the key idea in one sentence.
  3. Do the observation exercise, even if it is only watching a roasting video or comparing two roasted coffees.
  4. Answer the reflection prompt in your own words.
  5. Check the "Before you continue" box at the end of the page.

Good self-study habits

  • Change one major variable at a time.
  • Do not judge by curve alone. Taste the coffee.
  • Compare coffees side by side when possible.
  • Keep notes short enough that you will actually repeat them.
  • Use references to clarify terms, not to replace practice.
