Quality and Beginner-Friendliness Notes

These notes summarize the content scan before page generation. The lesson pages include caution boxes and checkpoints based on this review.

Main findings

  • Ratios, grind settings, water temperatures, and brew times should be framed as starting points, not rules.
  • Beginner safety needs emphasis around hot water, glass breakage, steam, espresso pressure, electrical equipment, and cleaning chemicals.
  • Espresso and latte art lessons are advanced for independent learners and need reminders to master basics first.
  • Extraction troubleshooting should avoid one-cause diagnosis; sour, bitter, thin, and harsh cups can come from several interacting variables.
  • Water chemistry guidance should protect equipment as well as taste and should avoid casual mineral additions without measurement.

Editing approach

Keep the original teaching context, but avoid rigid claims where coffee depends on equipment, water, roast level, palate, environment, or repeated tasting.
