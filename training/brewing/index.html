The loop

Brew. Taste. Diagnose. Change one thing. Record.

Recipes are starting points.

The cup tells you what to adjust.

Change one thing.

Grind, ratio, time, water, agitation, or temperature.

Write it down.

Brewing skill grows through repeatable notes.
