Roastery

Coffee roasted in small batches, mostly to order.

We look first for good coffees from careful Indian producers, and we stay open to good coffees from elsewhere when they make sense. The aim is simple: roast with respect for the grower, then help you brew it well.

  • Origin, producer, process, roast date, and brew suggestion should be easy to see.
  • If a coffee is limited, seasonal, or unavailable, we should say that plainly.
  • Ordering and brew questions should stay easy to ask.

Buna

Buna is the deeper library.

Buna follows coffee leaf through living traditions, sensory learning, processing experiments, vocabulary, culinary use, and evidence notes. Start lightly here, then enter the full library when you want the long path.

Buna overview Full library

Citane

Citane is where the leaf becomes a drink.

Citane is our coffee leaf processing work: harvest, wither, roast, dry, brew, taste, log, and revise. It is experimental, but not vague. The pleasure is in the cup, and the honesty is in the notes.
