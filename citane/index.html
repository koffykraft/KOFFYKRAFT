Citane

Coffee leaf processing, logged and tasted.

Citane is KoffyKraft's practical coffee leaf project: harvest, wither, roast or dry, brew, taste, record, and improve. It should be inviting, but not loose with claims.

Process

Leaf to cup

Citane should show the route from picking and leaf selection to withering, thermal treatment, drying, storage, brewing, and sensory notes.

Public

Recipes and field notes

Keep public pages practical: how it is brewed, what it tastes like, what was tried, and what is still experimental.

Boundary

No careless health claims

Functional or nutrition notes need references and cautious wording. Product pages should focus on taste, process, and transparency.
